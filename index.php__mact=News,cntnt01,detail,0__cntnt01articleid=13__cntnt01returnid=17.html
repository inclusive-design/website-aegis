Print me a Stradivarius (or a better case for my mobile)

In a signal that rapid prototyping is beginning to reach the mainstream, The Economist magazine has published an article on rapid prototyping entitlled: Print me a Stradivarius How a new manufacturing technology will change the world (http://www.economist.com/node/18114327). The AEGIS (Ontario) project may soon include a rapid prototyping aspect with the participation of the IDI Mobile and Pervasive Research project at the University of Toronto.
